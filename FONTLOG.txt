Basic Font Information

Pixeltron Now is a digitized interpretation of the fonts seen on many calculators
and vintage games and software. Originally used for old-web website mocs, this style
can also be used for modern projects. Its monospace and monoheight aspects gives it the
ability to look uniform on any device, while maintaining consistency.
